<Step number="01" urdu="بنیاد" title="Google Business Profile — aap ki digital dukaan">
  <p>
    Agar aap ka business Pakistan mein hai aur aap ne Google Business Profile claim nahi
    kiya — aap apne competitor ko muft mein customers de rahe hain. Yeh aap ki "digital
    dukaan ka board" hai.
  </p>
  <ul class="space-y-2 pl-4 [&>li]:list-disc">
    <li>Complete address, working hours aur 5–10 quality photos add karein</li>
    <li>Primary category sahi chunein — "Restaurant" vs "Pakistani Restaurant" mein farq hota hai</li>
    <li>Har hafte ek post ya offer publish karein — Google ko activity dikhayein</li>
  </ul>
</Step>

<Step number="02" urdu="کلمات" title="Urdu + English keyword strategy">
  <p>
    Pakistani users dono zubaanon mein search karte hain — "biryani near me" aur "Karachi
    mein biryani" dono. Aap ki content strategy mein dono shamil hone chahiye, lekin
    transliterated Roman Urdu naturally use karein, forced nahi.
  </p>
  <p>
    Google Keyword Planner ke saath Urdu trends bhi check karein — aksar low-competition
    keywords mil jaate hain jo competitors miss kar dete hain.
  </p>
</Step>

<Step number="03" urdu="روابط" title="Local backlinks aur citations">
  <p>
    Local SEO ka 40% wazan backlinks aur citations par hai. Pakistani directories,
    chamber-of-commerce listings, aur local news mentions sab count hote hain.
  </p>
  <ul class="space-y-2 pl-4 [&>li]:list-disc">
    <li>Locanto, OLX Business, PakBiz, aur Yellow Pages Pakistan par list ho jayein</li>
    <li>City chamber of commerce ki membership consider karein</li>
    <li>Local bloggers ko free service de kar honest review request karein</li>
  </ul>
</Step>

<Step number="04" urdu="رفتار" title="Speed — Pakistan ka 4G test pass karein">
  <p>
    Aap ki site agar Lahore ke fiber connection par fast hai par Faisalabad ke 4G par 6
    seconds leti hai, aap aadhe customers kho rahe hain. Mobile-first, image compression,
    aur CDN — yeh teen cheezein non-negotiable hain.
  </p>
  <blockquote class="rounded-2xl border-l-4 border-accent bg-accent/10 p-6 font-serif text-xl italic text-brand-secondary">
    "Rs. 1 ka SEO mat karein agar aap ki site 4G par 3 second se zyada le rahi hai. Pehle
    speed, phir rankings."
  </blockquote>
</Step>

<Step number="05" urdu="جائزہ" title="Reviews — social proof ka asli khel">
  <p>
    Pakistani consumers reviews ko ziyada serious lete hain. Agar aap ke paas 50+ Google
    reviews hain 4.5+ rating ke saath, aap apni competition se dugna lead generate karein
    ge — bina kisi extra ad spend ke.
  </p>
  <p>
    Har satisfied customer ko WhatsApp par direct review link bhejein. Pre-written message
    template use karein taake kaam asaan ho.
  </p>
</Step>

<Step number="06" urdu="مواد" title="Content — sirf English mat likhein">
  <p>
    Roman Urdu aur English mix wala content Pakistani audience ke saath sab se zyada
    connect karta hai. "How to start a clothing brand in Pakistan" + "Pakistan mein
    clothing brand kaise shuru karein" — dono ke liye alag pages banayein.
  </p>
</Step>

<Step number="07" urdu="پیمائش" title="Measure karein, warna sab guesswork hai">
  <p>
    Google Search Console, Analytics, aur ek simple monthly report — bus. Agar aap ka SEO
    wala har mahine sirf "kaam ho raha hai" bolta hai bina numbers ke, woh aap ka waqt
    barbaad kar raha hai.
  </p>
</Step>
